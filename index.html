1 Data Layer Readiness

  • The biggest workload and bottleneck surfaced during the POC came from our internal data foundations, not from Presight's tool.
  • Our DA/DE teams still need more time to standardize datasets, metrics, and pipelines before adopting any advanced BI/AI tool.

2 Operational Overhead

  • Adopting Presight would introduce additional ongoing workload (semantic/metrics maintenance, user support) that current capacity cannot absorb.

3 BI Capabilities Not Fully Aligned

  • From a pure BI perspective, we need more advanced visualization & dashboard-building capabilities comparable to:
    • Tableau
    • ThinkingData
    • Superset (custom)
  • Current Presight BI interfaces do not yet match the flexibility and configurability required for our game operations dashboards.

4 AI Insights Not Deep Enough Yet

  • From an AI insights perspective, the generated insights are not yet at the depth or automation level required by our domain experts.
  • Insights still require manual interpretation, post-processing, or domain-specific context to be actionable.
